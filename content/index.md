# Interactive Signalling Laboratory (SigLab)

SigLab is a hands-on learning system for railway signalling. This site is the public portal for the project and points to the canonical repositories that provide raw engineering and teaching assets.

## Start here

- Read the [concept](concept.md) to understand goals, audience, and learning outcomes.
- Go to [build it](build-it/index.md) for construction and material entry points.
- Go to [play it](play-it/index.md) for rules and scenario material.
- Go to [teach with it](teach-with-it/index.md) for session planning and facilitation notes.
- Use [downloads](downloads.md) for direct PDFs and packaged assets.
